Privacy Policy

Welcome to Fai, your go-to meme generator! Your privacy is very important to us. This document outlines how we collect, use, and handle your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We only collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Text messages: Any text messages that you input into the app for generating memes.
  • Generated memes: The memes you create using the app.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the app experience. Specifically:

  • To generate and store your memes temporarily.
  • To allow you to access your previously created memes within the 30-day retention period.

Data Retention

All text messages and generated memes are stored in our database for a maximum of 30 days. After this period, all stored data is permanently deleted from our servers.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data before the 30-day period by contacting us directly at connect@faiapp.com.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically for any changes. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at connect@faiapp.com.

Thank you for using Fai!
